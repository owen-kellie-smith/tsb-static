<h2>Privacy Policy</h2>

<p>We undertake to preserve the confidentiality of all information provided to us. At Thornbury
Swing Band, we only process information necessary to establish or maintain details of those who
play in the band and support the band, and to carry out our sole function as a not-for-profit
community band.</p>

<p>Specifically we will:</p>
<ul class="info-list">
  <li>Only process information necessary to provide or administer activities for people who play
  in or have regular contact with Thornbury Swing Band, or who engage our services as a band.</li>
  <li>Only hold information about individuals that is necessary for the above.</li>
  <li>Restrict personal data to what is necessary for the above.</li>
</ul>

<p>To the best of our knowledge, our policy complies with the Data Protection Act 2018 and the
EU General Data Protection Regulation.</p>

<h2>Cookie Policy</h2>

<p>This website does not use cookies or any form of tracking to identify who visits our site.</p>
