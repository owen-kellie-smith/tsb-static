**We’ll do everything we can to make your event a success!**

As every event is different, we will work together with you to understand your requirements.  
Our bassist Luigi will be your lead point of contact. Luigi will liaise with you, ensure we provide the very best experience for your audience, and be your on-the-night contact too.

Check out our information for event organisers below. And if you’re looking for a band for a wedding, take a look further below at how we can help.

## Information for Event Organisers

- **The Band:** We are a full big band with 5 saxophones, 4 trumpets, 4 trombones, rhythm section and vocalists – 19 people in total. For smaller venues or additional music we also have a 4–8 person band.

- **The Music:** We play a wide range of swing and dance music from the 1930s to the present day and will tailor the music for your event. For example we will provide more Glenn Miller music for a 1940s dance, or include more modern swing for a contemporary event. See our [current repertoire (PDF)](../assets/thornbury-swing-band-current-repertoire.pdf){:target="_blank"}.

- **Duration:** We are flexible about how long and when we play and will fit in with your plan for the evening. We typically provide two sets of 45–50 minutes which fits well when there are other performers or activities.

- **Dancing:** We have worked in partnership with a local dance teacher who provides basic swing and jive dance tuition and demonstrations at the start of the evening. Great for getting everyone in the mood!

- **Space:** A big band requires quite a big stage space. Around 8m×5m is an absolute minimum, bigger is better. We also require power and lighting to the stage. We are happy to come to look at a venue’s space in advance.

- **Outdoor Venues:** We are happy to play outdoors. We ask that event organisers provide a marquee/gazebo of at least 8m×5m with side covers, and our PA must be protected from rain at all times.

- **Publicity:** We will support you in publicising and marketing your event. We have a loyal group of followers who attend most of our events and we will publicise your event on our website and through social media.

- **Fees:** There is no standard fee as this depends on the event type and location – please [get in touch](../contact/) to discuss options. We offer substantially reduced rates for charity events. In addition to our fee we ask event organisers to pay the actual costs of us providing our own PA system and sound engineer (see below). This is usually around £200 although it can be up to £500 if our preferred supplier is not available. Booking a long way in advance helps to secure the best price.

- **PA:** We will provide our own trusted PA system and sound engineer. If your venue has a professional sound system that can deal with a full big band (3–6 instrument mikes, 4 vocal mikes, 2 monitor speakers etc.) we can use that instead.

- **Food & Drink:** While the band doesn’t expect to be fed, it is very much appreciated – especially as our band members do not get paid to play!

- **Insurance:** The band has Third Party Liability Insurance and can provide the certificate if needed.

- **Liaison:** Luigi Caludi will be your lead point of contact with the band. He will work with you to understand your requirements and make sure we provide the best possible solution for your event. We do need at least one formal meeting before the event.

- **Next Steps:** Every event is different so please [get in touch](../contact/) to discuss your specific requirements.

This [information for event organisers is also available for download](../assets/useful_information_for_event_organisers.pdf).

## Weddings

![Wedding party dancing](../assets/uploads/2021/08/wedding-party-dance-bride-groom-fun-celebration-539409d.jpg2dff.jpg "Wedding party dancing")

For a wedding we can provide:

- Our own high-quality PA
- A first dance performance from our repertoire
- DJ of a first dance request if not from our repertoire
- 2×60-minute sets of live big band music

**The full band** (17-strong classic big band) can deliver a full evening of entertainment.  
**The small band** is ideal for smaller venues or where the full band would be too large.
