We promise that we won’t sell, share or let anyone else have your personal details – see our privacy policy below.

Privacy Policy

We undertake to preserve the confidentiality of all information provided to us. At Thornbury Swing Band, we only process information necessary to establish or maintain details of those who play in the band and support the band, and to carry out our sole function as a not-for-profit community band.

Specifically we will:

  • Only process information necessary to provide or administer activities for people who play in or have regular contact with Thornbury Swing Band, or who engage our services as a band.
  • Only hold information about individuals that is necessary for the above.
  • Restrict personal data to what is necessary for the above.

To the best of our knowledge, our policy complies with the Data Protection Act 2018 and the EU General Data Protection Regulation.

Cookie Policy
